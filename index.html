About Billionaire NFTs

Founded by artist and author Arthur Palyan, Billionaire NFTs is a digital marketplace for innovative, collectible art. The brand also works with aspiring digital art entrepreneurs to make their monetization or collectible dreams a reality. Collectors and investors can find Billionaire NFTs on the OpenSea platform.
